## What you can do with Previa

<Steps>
  <Step title="Scan prediction markets">
    Browse live markets from Kalshi and Polymarket in one unified view. Filter by category, platform, or sort by volume, price movement, or AI score.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read AI intelligence">
    Every market gets a composite confidence score powered by real-time price data, news analysis, and sentiment signals. Pro and Premium users get full score breakdowns and reasoning.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track your portfolio">
    Link your Kalshi or Polymarket accounts to see your open positions, P&L, and portfolio summary in one place.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay ahead with alerts">
    Set alerts for price thresholds, score shifts, news catalysts, or volume spikes. Receive notifications via push, email, or in-app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Coming soon

These features are on the roadmap and will be added to the platform:

- **AI research reports** — Deep-dive analysis generated by AI for individual markets or events (Premium)
- **Social intelligence dashboard** — Real-time sentiment from X, Reddit, and Telegram with anomaly detection
- **Cross-market correlations** — Identify statistically linked markets for hedging and discovery
- **Daily/weekly market digests** — AI-generated briefings of top movers, news drivers, and anomalies
- **Additional platforms** — Manifold, Metaculus, and PredictIt coverage
- **Public developer API** — Programmatic access to market data, scores, and alerts
