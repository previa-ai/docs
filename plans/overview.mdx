## Free plan

The Free plan gives you access to the core market scanner with live prices across Kalshi and Polymarket. You get:

- Full market scanner with search, filters, and sorting
- Real-time price charts on market detail pages
- 3 watchlist items
- 1 AI insight per day (composite score visible, breakdown gated)
- 5 alert rules
- Community access

No credit card required.

## Pro plan

Pro unlocks the AI intelligence layer and portfolio tracking. Best for active traders who want data-driven insights.

Everything in Free, plus:

- **Unlimited AI insights** with full score breakdowns and AI reasoning
- **News-to-market mapping** — see which news articles are driving scores
- **50 smart alerts** across all trigger types
- **Portfolio tracking** — connect Kalshi and Polymarket accounts
- **Unlimited watchlist**
- **Priority support**

## Premium plan

Premium adds social intelligence, research reports, and API access. Best for professional traders and research teams.

Everything in Pro, plus:

- **Social intelligence** — Real-time sentiment from X, Reddit, and Telegram with anomaly detection
- **AI research reports** — Deep-dive analysis generated for individual markets or events
- **Unlimited alerts**
- **Limited API access** — Programmatic access to market data and scores
- **Custom watchlists and categories**

AI research reports and the full social intelligence dashboard are coming soon. Premium subscribers will get access as these features launch.
