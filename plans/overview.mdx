## Free plan

The Free plan gives you access to the core market scanner with live prices across Kalshi and Polymarket. You get:

- Full market scanner with search, filters, and sorting
- Real-time price charts on market detail pages
- 5 watchlist items
- 50 AI insights per month (composite score visible, breakdown gated)
- 5 alert rules (all trigger types)
- Community access

No credit card required.

## Pro plan

Pro unlocks the AI intelligence layer, social signals, and portfolio tracking. Best for active traders who want data-driven insights.

Everything in Free, plus:

- **200 AI insights per month** with full score breakdowns and AI reasoning
- **News-to-market mapping** — see which news articles are driving scores
- **Social intelligence** — real-time sentiment from X, Reddit, and Telegram with anomaly detection
- **5 AI deep research reports per month** — deep-dive analysis generated for individual markets or events
- **Unlimited smart alerts** across all trigger types
- **Portfolio tracking** — connect Kalshi and Polymarket accounts
- **Portfolio insights** — AI-powered recommendations on when to double down or exit positions
- **Unlimited watchlist**
- **Custom watchlists and categories**

## Enterprise plan

Custom pricing for teams, hedge funds, research firms, and media organizations. Includes:

- **AI research reports with credit packs** — 20 reports/month included, additional packs of 25, 50, or 100 available
- **Unlimited AI insights and confidence scores**
- **Custom AI models and scoring** tailored to your use case
- **White-label intelligence** — license the scoring engine for your platform
- **Priority support and dedicated account manager**
- **SLA with 99.9% uptime guarantee**
- **Custom integrations**
- **Team management** — multi-seat access with organization-level billing
