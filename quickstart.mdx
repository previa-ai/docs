This guide walks you through creating your account, exploring your first market, and setting an alert.

## 1. Create your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Previa">
    Go to [app.useprevia.com](https://app.useprevia.com) on your browser, or download the Previa app from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com) or [Google Play](https://play.google.com).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Choose one of:
    - **Email** — Enter your email and follow the verification link sent to your inbox.
    - **Google** — Sign in with your Google account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    Select at least 3 categories you're interested in (Politics, Crypto, Sports, etc.). These personalize your insights feed. You can change them later in Settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your account is ready. You're on the **Free plan** by default, which includes the market scanner, 3 watchlist items, 1 daily AI insight, and 5 alerts.

## 2. Browse markets

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Markets tab">
    The Markets tab is your home screen. It shows live prediction markets from Kalshi and Polymarket.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter markets">
    Use the filter chips at the top to narrow results:
    - **Category** — Politics, Economics, Crypto, Sports, and more.
    - **Platform** — All, Kalshi, or Polymarket.
    - **Sort** — Volume, Newest, Closing Soon, or AI Score.

    Type in the search bar to find a specific market by keyword.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap a market">
    Tap any market card to open its detail page. You'll see the current YES/NO price, a price chart, volume, and the AI confidence score.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 3. Save a market to your watchlist

On any market detail page, tap the **heart icon** to add it to your watchlist. Switch to the **Watchlist** segment on the Markets tab to see your saved markets.

## 4. Set your first alert

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Alerts tab">
    Tap the Alerts tab in the bottom navigation (mobile) or sidebar (desktop).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Create Alert">
    Choose the market you want to monitor, then configure a trigger:
    - **Price threshold** — Alert when YES or NO price crosses a value.
    - **Score shift** — Alert when the AI confidence score changes by a set amount.
    - **News catalyst** — Alert when high-impact news is matched to the market.
    - **Volume spike** — Alert when trading volume surges.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose delivery">
    Select where you want to receive the alert: push notification, email, or both. Tap **Create** to save.
  </Step>
</Steps>
