Previa aggregates market data from multiple prediction market platforms into a single interface. Each platform has different data availability and connection options.

## Kalshi

[Kalshi](https://kalshi.com) is a US-regulated prediction market exchange (CFTC-regulated). Markets cover politics, economics, weather, tech, and more.

**What Previa pulls from Kalshi:**
- Live market listings and metadata
- Real-time YES/NO prices
- 24-hour volume and liquidity
- Historical OHLCV price data (candlesticks)
- Market resolution status

**Portfolio tracking:** Connect your Kalshi account using your API key and secret to track open positions and P&L. Available on Pro and Premium plans.

## Polymarket

[Polymarket](https://polymarket.com) is a decentralized prediction market built on Polygon. It's one of the largest prediction markets by volume.

**What Previa pulls from Polymarket:**
- Live market listings and metadata
- Real-time YES/NO prices via the CLOB API
- Volume and liquidity data
- Historical price data

**Portfolio tracking:** Connect your Polymarket wallet address to track positions. Available on Pro and Premium plans.

<Note>
Polymarket portfolio tracking is being improved. Currently, the connection validates your wallet address and fetches positions from the public CLOB API. Full on-chain USDC position reads are on the roadmap.
</Note>
